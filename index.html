PROJ-005 IN PROGRESS JUN–SEP 2026

Vibration Isolation Module

Passive parallel-link isolation stage for a 2.5 kg camera/LiDAR payload on a delivery robot. Designed from a validated SDOF model (fn = 3.5 Hz, ζ ≈ 0.12), targeting ≥40% RMS acceleration reduction over the 5–30 Hz band, verified with a dual-accelerometer Arduino test rig.

Specifications & approach
MODELMATLAB SDOF — transmissibility, terrain PSD response, bump response
STRUCTUREParallel 4-bar linkage, waterjet 6061 Al, ±15 mm stroke, k = 1200 N/m
ANALYSISANSYS static (3g, SF ≥ 2) + modal (structural modes > 50 Hz)
VALIDATION2× MPU6050 + Arduino DAQ; log-decrement & broadband PSD comparison

PROJ-004 COMPLETE APR 2025 – 2026

Micro Pin-Fin Heatsink Simulation — CTRC

End-to-end thermal simulation pipelines in ANSYS Fluent and Mechanical at Purdue's Cooling Technologies Research Center: geometry, meshing, solver setup, and post-processing for high-heat-flux pin-fin arrays. Cut simulation turnaround ~35% by validating reduced-order 2D and symmetry models against full 3D baselines.

Specifications & approach
DOMAINConjugate heat transfer + pressure drop, micro pin-fin arrays
TOOLSANSYS Fluent (CFD), ANSYS Mechanical (FEA)
METHODMesh-resolution studies; 2D vs 3D and symmetry-layout trade studies
OUTCOME~35% lower computational cost at preserved accuracy
