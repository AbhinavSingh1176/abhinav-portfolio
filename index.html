Experience

Undergraduate Researcher

Cooling Technologies Research Center, Purdue University • 2025 - Present

As an Undergraduate Researcher, I've conducted end-to-end simulations using ANSYS Fluent to analyze heat transfer and pressure drop in pin-fin heatsink geometries. I've performed comparative thermal analysis on different pin-fin orientations, evaluating performance and accuracy tradeoffs. This role has provided me with invaluable experience in computational fluid dynamics (CFD) and thermal management research.

ME Research Intern

Maharaja Sayajirao University, Vadodara • July 2023 - August 2023

During my internship, I conducted full-time research on parabolic solar concentrators and thermocouple-based heat flux sensors. I drafted a thesis manuscript and led data acquisition to experimentally validate thermal conductivity and sensor sensitivity. This experience exposed me to academic research methodologies, patent drafting, and intellectual property strategies through collaboration with faculty and graduate researchers.

Powertrains Team Member

ASME Racing, Purdue University • 2025 - Present

I designed Purdue's first in-house CVT mount using Autodesk Fusion, optimizing packaging and transmission efficiency. I also engineered and fabricated a custom metal shroud for heat protection and a repositionable exhaust mount to correct misalignment. This hands-on experience has been crucial in developing my skills in CAD, fabrication, and problem-solving in a fast-paced team environment.

Project Archivist, Kart Manual Team

EPICS, Purdue University • 2025

As Project Archivist, I developed a beginner-friendly kart assembly manual for high school students participating in the Purdue EV Grand Prix. I collaborated with the Director of Purdue Motorsports and TopKart USA to create instructional templates and integrated CAD visuals from SolidWorks to support hands-on learning. My efforts significantly reduced kart assembly time and improved the learning experience for participants.
