Vibration Isolation Module

VIM-001 · PASSIVE PARALLEL-LINK STAGE · IN PROGRESS

A passive isolation stage for the 2.5 kg camera and LiDAR payload on a delivery robot. The goal is simple to state and hard to earn: cut RMS acceleration by 40% or more across the 5–30 Hz band, and prove it with hardware rather than a slide. This page is the running log, updated as each phase clears.

01 SPECIFICATIONS

PAYLOAD2.5 kg camera + LiDAR package on a last-mile delivery robot
TARGET≥ 40% RMS acceleration reduction across the 5–30 Hz band
MODELMATLAB SDOF: transmissibility, terrain PSD response, bump response (fn = 3.5 Hz, ζ ≈ 0.12)
STRUCTUREParallel 4-bar linkage, waterjet 6061 Al, ±15 mm stroke, k = 1200 N/m
ANALYSISANSYS static (3g, SF ≥ 2) + modal (structural modes > 50 Hz)
VALIDATION2× MPU6050 + Arduino DAQ; log-decrement and broadband PSD comparison
